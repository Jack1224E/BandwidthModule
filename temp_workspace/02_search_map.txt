1.  **The Rarity Bootstrap (libtorrent/src/piece_picker.cpp)**:
    - **Signature**: `void piece_picker::inc_refcount(typed_bitfield<piece_index_t> const& bitmask, torrent_peer const* peer)`
    - **Usage**: Iterates over the bitmask; for each bit set, it updates `m_piece_map[piece].peer_count` and moves pieces between rarity buckets in `m_priority_boundaries`.

2.  **Peer Stubs (libtorrent/include/libtorrent/torrent_peer.hpp)**:
    - **Minimum Fields**:
        - `peer_connection_interface* connection`: Dereferenced to check if the peer is still active.
        - `bool seed`: Used to skip rarity updates if the peer has everything (seeds are tracked separately in `m_seeds`).
        - `bool on_parole`: Affects piece picking priority (prioritizes partials).
        - `bool banned`: Must be checked before assigning blocks.

3.  **The Modulo Bridge (aria2/src/SegmentMan.cc)**:
    - **Handling**: `SegmentMan` currently delegates to `PieceStorage::getMissingPiece`.
    - **Intercept Point**: `SegmentMan::getSegment` (Line 203) and `SegmentMan::getSegmentWithIndex` (Line 245). Replacing these calls with `LTPiecePicker::pick_pieces()` ensures our modulo math controls the initial allocation.

4.  **End-Game Sniping (libtorrent/src/peer_connection.cpp)**:
    - **Mechanism**: libtorrent maintains a `m_outstanding_requests` queue. When a `piece` message arrives, it calls `picker.mark_as_finished()`. If this completes a piece, it iterates over all other peers and calls `peer->cancel_request(block)` for any redundant requests.

5.  **The Hashing Trigger (aria2/src/PieceStorage.cc)**:
    - **Method**: `DefaultPieceStorage::completePiece` (Line 459) is where aria2 marks a piece as "done" in its bitfield.
    - **Gating**: We must intercept this call to ensure `LTPiecePicker` has moved the piece to the final terminal state *only* after `PieceStorage` confirms the hash.

6.  **Ceiling Math (libtorrent/src/piece_picker.cpp)**:
    - **Verification**: `Line 173` uses `((total_size % piece_size) + block_size() - 1) / block_size()`. 
    - **Conclusion**: Our `last_bpp` formula is 100% compliant with libtorrent internals.
